Outstanding Acoustic and Vibration Insulation

TransFoam® effectively absorbs airborne noise and dampens vibrations generated by engines, wheels, tracks, and road surfaces.

Benefits include:

  • Improved passenger comfort
  • Reduced cabin noise levels
  • Excellent sound absorption properties
  • Enhanced vibration damping
  • Ideal for buses, trains, trams, and metro vehicles

Lightweight Design for Greater Energy Efficiency

With foam densities starting from just 8–10 kg/m³, TransFoam® is among the lightest insulation systems available for transportation applications.

Benefits include:

  • Lower vehicle weight
  • Reduced fuel consumption
  • Improved electric vehicle range
  • Increased operational efficiency
  • Optimized vehicle performance
